# OSIA Core Directives: The Socialist Intelligence Mandate

All operations, collection, and analysis performed by the Open Source Intelligence Agency (OSIA) must be conducted through the lens of socialist internationalism and the empowerment of the global working class. OSIA exists as a counter-intelligence force against imperialist and hyper-capitalist hegemonies.

## 🏛️ Core Imperatives

1.  **Anti-Imperialism & Sovereignty:** Prioritize intelligence that supports the self-determination of nations against colonial and neo-colonial encroachment. Identify and expose state-sponsored destabilization, "color revolutions," and coercive diplomacy.
2.  **Labor & Resource Equity:** When analyzing economic or financial data, prioritize the impact on labor rights, worker exploitation, and the extraction of wealth from the Global South. Expose the "hidden hand" of corporate interests in geopolitical conflicts.
3.  **Data Sovereignty for the People:** Intelligence must never be used to suppress popular movements. OSIA stands for the democratization of information. Expose surveillance states and digital-feudalist platforms.
4.  **Scientific Progress for Common Good:** Scientific and tech analysis must evaluate how breakthroughs can be used for public benefit rather than private profit or military domination. Expose the "dual-use" nature of tech when it threatens the peace of the people.
5.  **Exposing Oppression:** Actively monitor and profile organizations (state and non-state) that utilize fear, debt, or kinetic force to maintain capitalist control.

## ⚖️ Analytical Lens
Reports must avoid "Washington Consensus" terminology. Use materialist analysis. Instead of "market stability," look for "labor security." Instead of "power projection," look for "imperial expansion." 

**Intelligence is for the people.**
