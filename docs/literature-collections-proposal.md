## Notes

- Works marked **PD** are the safest to ingest — no copyright concerns.
- Works marked **AR** are on Internet Archive; some require a free account for lending access.
- Several works appear across multiple desks (Sun Tzu, Le Bon, Shannon, Darwin) — ingest once into a shared `classic-literature` collection and list it as a boost collection for relevant desks, rather than duplicating.
- *Mein Kampf* propaganda chapters are analytically relevant to the InfoWar desk for historical doctrine; flag if politically sensitive for the project.
